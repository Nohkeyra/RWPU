# Restoran Wawasan Ad Campaign & Video Assets 🎥🚀

Welcome to your **Advertisement Campaign Folder**! This folder has been structured to provide you with all the necessary creative and copywriting resources to create a professional advertisement video and promotional campaign for your app.

## 📂 Folder Structure

- 📝 **`video_script_en.md`**: A timed 60-second professional video script in English. It features voiceovers, visual concepts, text overlays, and audio cues to help you film or generate an eye-catching video ad.
- 📝 **`video_script_bm.md`**: A localized Bahasa Melayu version of the 60-second video script, optimized for local corporate and private event organizers.
- 📝 **`marketing_copy.md`**: A set of social media caption templates (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram/TikTok, and WhatsApp Business) and strategic target audience profiles to help you launch the campaign successfully.

---

## 💡 Quick Tips for Creating Your Video:

1. **Free Mockup Tools**: Use free online device mockup generators (like Smartmockups or Canva) to place high-quality screen recordings of your app into a sleek mobile frame.
2. **AI Voiceovers**: If you are not recording the voiceover yourself, you can use high-quality AI voiceover tools (such as ElevenLabs or Speechelo) with a professional corporate or warm friendly tone.
3. **Background Music**: Choose energetic, upbeat, yet clean corporate-acoustic or lo-fi electronic background music so it doesn't distract from the narration.
4. **Captions**: Always add captions/sari kata directly inside the video since over 70% of viewers scroll social media with their audio muted!
