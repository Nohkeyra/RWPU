## Production & Shooting Tips

1. **High Contrast Lighting:** When shooting food close-ups, use soft directional side-lighting to make the dishes look rich and appetizing.
2. **Mobile Device Mockup:** For the app walkthrough parts, use a high-resolution smartphone layout with dark mode to match the app's premium dark charcoal/warm gold aesthetics.
3. **Pacing:** Keep the transitions snappy! Mobile users scroll quickly, so make sure the first 3 seconds grab attention instantly with high-quality food visuals and a relatable pain point.
